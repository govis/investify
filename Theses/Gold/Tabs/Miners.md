### **Strategic Institutional Context**

Institutional capital typically flows top-down, starting with senior producers before filtering into the junior sector. For 2026, the "funnel" is filling rapidly as senior miners' market caps have nearly doubled since 2024, driving value toward developers like Skeena and high-conviction explorers like Snowline Gold, which offer significant re-rating potential upon hitting technical milestones.
