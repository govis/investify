# The Nuclear Energy Renaissance to Support Growing Electricity Demands
Image: Thesis 3.jpg

### Executive Summary

The acceleration of global electrification and the immense, continuous power requirements of AI hyperscale data centers have catalyzed a historic renaissance in nuclear energy.19 Recognized universally as the only scalable, dispatchable, zero-carbon baseload power source capable of complementing the intermittency of renewable energy, nuclear capacity is undergoing a rapid, policy-supported global expansion.20 However, the industry's growth trajectory is severely constrained by supply chain bottlenecks resulting from decades of stagnation. An impending structural deficit in mined uranium and a critical shortage of specialized enriched fuel create distinct, high-conviction value-capture opportunities across the upstream mining sector, established utility operators, and next-generation reactor developers.21
