# Invesco Aerospace & Defense ETF

**Website:** [invesco.com](https://www.invesco.com/us/en/solutions/exchange-traded-funds/ppa.html)
**Logo:** https://www.invesco.com/us/en/assets/images/logo-invesco.svg
**Type:** Aerospace & Defense ETF (Defense)
**Country:** USA

The Invesco Aerospace & Defense ETF (PPA) offers diversified exposure to the U.S. aerospace and defense industry, including companies involved in homeland security and space operations. The fund tracks the SPADE Defense Index and utilizes a modified market-cap weighting system to prevent over-concentration in any single constituent. PPA provides investors with a broad-based investment in the companies responsible for the development, production, and support of advanced defense systems and national security infrastructure.
