# Flowserve Corporation

**Website:** [flowserve.com](https://www.flowserve.com)
**Logo:** https://www.flowserve.com/static/images/logo.png
**Type:** Nuclear-Grade Components & Flow Control (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

Flowserve is a global leader in flow control solutions, manufacturing high-performance pumps, valves, and mechanical seals for critical industrial applications. The company is a major supplier of nuclear-grade components for both commercial power plants and naval reactors. Flowserve’s specialized expertise and its acquisition of nuclear-focused assets (like Trillium) position it as a key beneficiary of the increased investment in nuclear EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) and the modernization of global electricity grids.
