# Aura Energy Limited

**Website:** [auraenergy.com.au](https://www.auraenergy.com.au)
**Logo:** https://www.auraenergy.com.au/wp-content/themes/aura/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium & Polymetallic Development (Nuclear)
**Country:** Australia

Aura Energy is an Australian company transitioning from explorer to producer with a focus on its Tiris Uranium Project in Mauritania. Tiris is a low-capex, high-margin project with significant resource expansion potential. Aura also holds the Häggån Polymetallic Project in Sweden, which hosts a large-scale vanadium and uranium resource. The company provides investors with exposure to multiple critical minerals essential for the global energy transition and clean energy generation.
