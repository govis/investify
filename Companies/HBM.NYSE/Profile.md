# Hudbay Minerals Inc.

**Website:** [hudbayminerals.com](https://www.hudbayminerals.com)
**Logo:** https://www.hudbayminerals.com/wp-content/themes/hudbay/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Copper & Zinc Mining (Electrification)
**Country:** Canada

Hudbay Minerals is a diversified mining company primarily focused on copper production with long-life operations in Canada and Peru. The company is also advancing a significant pipeline of copper growth projects in the United States, including the Copper Mountain expansion and the Rosemont project. Hudbay is a mid-tier producer that offers attractive leverage to copper prices and the ongoing shift toward Western-allied mineral supply.
