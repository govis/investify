# Curtiss-Wright Corporation

**Website:** [curtisswright.com](https://www.curtisswright.com)
**Logo:** https://www.curtisswright.com/static/images/logo.png
**Type:** Nuclear Components & Engineering (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

Curtiss-Wright is an integrated global business providing highly engineered products and services to the aerospace, defense, and energy markets. The company is a strategic supplier of critical components for nuclear submarines and commercial nuclear power plants, including advanced reactor coolant pumps and control systems. Curtiss-Wright’s deep technical expertise and its long-standing relationship with the nuclear industry make it an essential player in the revitalization of the nuclear supply chain.
