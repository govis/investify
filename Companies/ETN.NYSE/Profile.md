# Eaton Corporation plc

**Website:** [eaton.com](https://www.eaton.com)
**Logo:** https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e7/Eaton_logo.svg/1280px-Eaton_logo.svg.png
**Type:** Electrical Equipment & Power Management (AI & Electrification)
**Country:** Ireland / USA

Eaton is a global intelligent power management company providing energy-efficient solutions for electrical, hydraulic, and mechanical power. The company is a primary beneficiary of the global trends in electrification, digitalization, and energy transition. Eaton provides critical power infrastructure for hyperscale data centers, including switchgear, UPS systems, and power distribution units, essential for the AI-driven compute surge. Its grid modernization and eMobility solutions also position it as a key "picks and shovels" provider for the global shift toward electric vehicles and renewable energy.
