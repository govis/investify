# Uranium Royalty Corp.

**Website:** [uraniumroyalty.com](https://www.uraniumroyalty.com)
**Logo:** https://www.uraniumroyalty.com/_resources/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Royalty & Streaming (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

Uranium Royalty Corp. is the world’s first and only pure-play uranium royalty company. It focuses on gaining exposure to uranium prices by making strategic investments in uranium interests, including royalties, streams, debt, and equity investments in uranium companies, as well as through direct holdings of physical uranium. The company owns a geographically diversified portfolio of royalties on world-class uranium projects across Canada, the U.S., Namibia, and Spain, providing investors with leveraged exposure to the uranium market without direct operational risks.
