# Energy Fuels Inc.

**Website:** [energyfuels.com](https://www.energyfuels.com)
**Logo:** https://www.energyfuels.com/wp-content/themes/energyfuels/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium & Rare Earth Elements (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

Energy Fuels is the leading U.S. producer of uranium and a major producer of vanadium. The company is also emerging as a global leader in the production of advanced rare earth element (REE) materials at its White Mesa Mill in Utah. Energy Fuels’ unique capability to process both uranium and rare earths makes it a critical strategic asset for U.S. energy and supply chain security, benefiting from both the nuclear renaissance and the reshoring of critical minerals.
