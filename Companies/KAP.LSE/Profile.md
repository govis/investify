# NAC Kazatomprom JSC

**Website:** [kazatomprom.kz](https://www.kazatomprom.kz)
**Logo:** https://www.kazatomprom.kz/themes/kazatomprom/img/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Mining & Fuel Cycle (Nuclear)
**Country:** Kazakhstan

Kazatomprom is the world's largest producer and seller of natural uranium, accounting for approximately 20% of total global primary uranium production. As the national atomic company of Kazakhstan, it operates a vast portfolio of low-cost in-situ recovery (ISR) mines. Kazatomprom is a dominant force in the global uranium market, providing critical supply to nuclear utilities worldwide and maintaining a strategic leadership position in the nuclear fuel cycle.
