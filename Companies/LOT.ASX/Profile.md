# Lotus Resources Limited

**Website:** [lotusresources.com.au](https://www.lotusresources.com.au)
**Logo:** https://www.lotusresources.com.au/wp-content/themes/lotus/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Mining (Nuclear)
**Country:** Australia

Lotus Resources is an advanced uranium company focused on the Kayelekera Uranium Project in Malawi. Kayelekera is a proven producer that is being advanced toward a restart to take advantage of the strengthening uranium market. Lotus also holds the Letlhakane Uranium Project in Botswana, one of the largest undeveloped uranium resources in the world. The company provides institutional investors with high-convexity exposure to the African uranium supply cycle.
