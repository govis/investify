# Lynas Rare Earths Ltd

**Website:** [lynasrareearths.com](https://lynasrareearths.com)
**Logo:** https://lynasrareearths.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/Lynas-Logo.png
**Type:** Rare Earth Mining & Processing (Reshoring)
**Country:** Australia

Lynas Rare Earths is the world’s largest producer of rare earths outside of China. It operates the high-grade Mt Weld mine in Western Australia and a major chemical processing facility in Kuantan, Malaysia. Lynas is a critical supplier of NdPr (neodymium and praseodymium) used in high-strength permanent magnets, essential for the global energy transition and advanced electronics.
