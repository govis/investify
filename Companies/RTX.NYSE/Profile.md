# RTX Corporation

**Website:** [rtx.com](https://www.rtx.com)
**Logo:** https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c5/RTX_logo.svg/1280px-RTX_logo.svg.png
**Type:** Aerospace & Defense Prime (Defense)
**Country:** USA

RTX Corporation (formerly Raytheon Technologies) is a multinational aerospace and defense conglomerate formed by the 2020 merger of United Technologies and Raytheon Company. The company operates through three industry-leading segments: Collins Aerospace (aerostructures and avionics), Pratt & Whitney (aircraft engines), and Raytheon (missile defense, satellites, and intelligence services). RTX holds a dominant position in both commercial aviation and defense, supported by a massive order backlog and critical involvement in high-priority programs such as the Tomahawk and AMRAAM missile systems.
