# Lockheed Martin Corporation

**Website:** [lockheedmartin.com](https://www.lockheedmartin.com)
**Logo:** https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/99/Lockheed_Martin_logo.svg/2560px-Lockheed_Martin_logo.svg.png
**Type:** Aerospace & Defense Prime (Defense)
**Country:** USA

Lockheed Martin is the world's largest defense contractor and a global leader in aerospace, security, and advanced technologies. The company operates through four primary business segments: Aeronautics, Missiles and Fire Control, Rotary and Mission Systems, and Space. As the primary contractor for major U.S. and allied programs like the F-35 Lightning II, Lockheed Martin benefits from long-term government contracts and a massive order backlog, providing stable and predictable revenue streams in an increasingly complex geopolitical environment.
