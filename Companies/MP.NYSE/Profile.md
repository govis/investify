# MP Materials Corp.

**Website:** [mpmaterials.com](https://mpmaterials.com)
**Logo:** https://mpmaterials.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/MP-Materials-Logo.png
**Type:** Rare Earth Mining & Processing (Reshoring)
**Country:** USA

MP Materials is the largest rare earth materials producer in the Western Hemisphere. It owns and operates the Mountain Pass mine in California, which is the only scaled rare earth mining and processing site in North America. The company is a strategic anchor for Western supply chain security, providing critical materials for electric vehicles, wind turbines, and defense applications.
