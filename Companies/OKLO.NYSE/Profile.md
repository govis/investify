# Oklo Inc.

**Website:** [oklo.com](https://oklo.com)
**Logo:** https://oklo.com/static/images/logo.png
**Type:** Advanced Nuclear Technology & SMRs (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

Oklo Inc. is a developer of advanced fission power plants designed to provide clean, affordable, and reliable energy. Its flagship "Aurora" powerhouse uses fast-fission technology and recycled nuclear fuel to generate carbon-free power. Oklo is a high-convexity play on the next generation of nuclear energy, focusing on localized applications such as data centers and industrial facilities where traditional large-scale nuclear may be impractical.
