# AtkinsRéalis

**Website:** [atkinsrealis.com](https://www.atkinsrealis.com)
**Logo:** https://www.atkinsrealis.com/static/images/atkinsrealis-logo.svg
**Type:** Nuclear Engineering & EPC Services (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

AtkinsRéalis (formerly SNC-Lavalin) is a global professional services and project management company and a leader in nuclear engineering. The company provides full-lifecycle services for nuclear power plants, including design, construction, maintenance, and decommissioning. As the steward of CANDU reactor technology, AtkinsRéalis is a critical player in the global nuclear supply chain, benefiting from the renewed interest in large-scale nuclear projects and the rehabilitation of nuclear infrastructure in allied markets.
