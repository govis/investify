# SQM (Sociedad Química y Minera de Chile)

**Website:** [sqm.com](https://www.sqm.com)
**Logo:** https://www.sqm.com/wp-content/themes/sqm/assets/img/logo-sqm.svg
**Type:** Lithium & Specialty Chemicals (Electrification)
**Country:** Chile

SQM is a global leader in the production of lithium, iodine, and specialty plant nutrients. Operating primarily in the Salar de Atacama in Chile, SQM has access to some of the world's highest-grade and lowest-cost lithium brine deposits. The company is a Tier 1 supplier to the global battery-electric ecosystem, providing high-purity lithium carbonate and lithium hydroxide essential for high-performance electric vehicle batteries.
