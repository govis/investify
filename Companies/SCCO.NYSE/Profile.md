# Southern Copper Corporation

**Website:** [southerncoppercorp.com](https://www.southerncoppercorp.com)
**Logo:** https://www.southerncoppercorp.com/images/logo.png
**Type:** Copper Mining & Processing (Electrification)
**Country:** USA / Mexico

Southern Copper Corporation is one of the largest integrated copper producers in the world and holds the industry's largest copper reserves. Majority-owned by Grupo México, the company operates major mining, smelting, and refining facilities in Peru and Mexico. Southern Copper is characterized by its lean cost structure and long-life assets, making it a high-quality defensive growth play within the global copper and electrification cycle.
