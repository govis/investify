# GE Vernova

**Website:** [gevernova.com](https://www.gevernova.com)
**Logo:** https://www.gevernova.com/content/dam/gepower-vernova/global/en_US/images/logos/ge-vernova-logo.svg
**Type:** Energy Infrastructure & Technology (Electrification)
**Country:** USA

GE Vernova is a purpose-built energy company spun off from General Electric, focusing on accelerating the path to reliable, affordable, and sustainable energy. The company operates across the entire energy value chain, including Power (gas, nuclear, hydro), Wind, and Electrification. GE Vernova is a critical player in the nuclear renaissance through its advanced reactor technologies, such as the BWRX-300 SMR, and its vast portfolio of grid transmission and distribution solutions.
