# Anfield Energy Inc.

**Website:** [anfieldenergy.com](https://anfieldenergy.com)
**Logo:** https://anfieldenergy.com/wp-content/themes/anfield/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium & Vanadium Development (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada / USA

Anfield Energy is a near-term uranium and vanadium producer focused on the development of its assets in the United States. A key strategic asset is the Shootaring Canyon Mill in Utah, one of only three licensed conventional uranium mills in the U.S. Anfield’s portfolio includes high-quality uranium and vanadium projects in Utah, Colorado, and Arizona, positioned to benefit from the growing demand for domestic nuclear fuel and energy storage solutions.
