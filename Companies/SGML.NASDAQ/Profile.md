# Sigma Lithium Corporation

**Website:** [sigmalithiumresources.com](https://www.sigmalithiumresources.com)
**Logo:** https://sigmalithiumcorp.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/logo-sigma.png
**Type:** Sustainable Lithium Production (Electrification)
**Country:** Canada / Brazil

Sigma Lithium is dedicated to producing environmentally sustainable, high-purity "Green Lithium" concentrate for the global electric vehicle battery supply chain. The company’s flagship Grota do Cirilo project in Brazil is one of the largest and highest-grade hard-rock lithium deposits in the world. Sigma Lithium utilizes 100% renewable energy and recycled water in its processing, setting a new global standard for sustainability in the critical minerals sector.
