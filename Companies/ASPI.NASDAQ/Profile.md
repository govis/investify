# ASP Isotopes Inc.

**Website:** [aspisotopes.com](https://www.aspisotopes.com)
**Logo:** https://www.aspisotopes.com/wp-content/themes/asp/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Isotope Enrichment & HALEU Fuel (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA / South Africa

ASP Isotopes is an isotope enrichment company utilizing advanced laser-based separation technology. The company is developing enrichment solutions for various high-value isotopes used in nuclear medicine, semiconductors, and next-generation nuclear fuel. Specifically, ASP Isotopes is targeting the production of High-Assay Low-Enriched Uranium (HALEU) to meet the critical fuel requirements of advanced nuclear reactors, providing a Western alternative to Russian enrichment services.
