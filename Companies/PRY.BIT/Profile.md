# Prysmian S.p.A.

**Website:** [prysmiangroup.com](https://www.prysmiangroup.com)
**Logo:** https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a1/Prysmian_Group_Logo.png/1200px-Prysmian_Group_Logo.png
**Type:** Energy & Telecom Cable Systems (Electrification)
**Country:** Italy

Prysmian S.p.A. is the world leader in the energy and telecom cable systems industry. Formed through the merger of Prysmian, Draka, and General Cable, the company designs, manufactures, and distributes cables and systems for power transmission, power distribution, and telecommunications. Prysmian is a primary beneficiary of the global energy transition, providing critical submarine and high-voltage underground cables for offshore wind farms and grid modernization, as well as fiber optic solutions for the digital transformation.
