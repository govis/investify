# iShares U.S. Aerospace & Defense ETF

**Website:** [ishares.com](https://www.ishares.com/us/products/239502/ishares-us-aerospace-defense-etf)
**Logo:** https://www.ishares.com/us/assets/images/logo/ishares-logo.svg
**Type:** Aerospace & Defense ETF (Defense)
**Country:** USA

The iShares U.S. Aerospace & Defense ETF (ITA) is the largest and most liquid exchange-traded fund in the aerospace and defense sector. It provides concentrated exposure to U.S. companies that manufacture commercial and military aircraft and other defense equipment. By tracking the Dow Jones U.S. Select Aerospace & Defense Index, ITA offers investors a market-cap-weighted gateway to the major defense "primes" and their extensive supply chains, benefiting from long-term government contracts and global re-armament cycles.
