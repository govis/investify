# Capstone Copper Corp.

**Website:** [capstonecopper.com](https://capstonecopper.com)
**Logo:** https://capstonecopper.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Copper Mining (Electrification)
**Country:** Canada

Capstone Copper is an Americas-focused copper producer with a portfolio of long-life mines and development projects in the United States, Mexico, and Chile. The company is focused on operational excellence and disciplined growth, positioning itself to benefit from the long-term structural deficit in the global copper market. Capstone's key assets include the Pinto Valley mine in Arizona and the Mantos Blancos and Mantoverde operations in Chile, providing high-quality exposure to the electrification super-cycle.
