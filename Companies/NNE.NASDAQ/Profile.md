# NANO Nuclear Energy Inc.

**Website:** [nanonuclearenergy.com](https://nanonuclearenergy.com)
**Logo:** https://nanonuclearenergy.com/wp-content/themes/nano/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Microreactors & Nuclear Technology (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

NANO Nuclear Energy is an advanced nuclear energy company developing portable, modular microreactors (ZEUS and KRONOS) designed for remote power applications and industrial use. The company is also working to build a vertically integrated nuclear fuel supply chain, including fuel fabrication and transportation. NANO Nuclear is a speculative, high-growth play on the "Compute Sovereignty" and decentralized power themes within the nuclear renaissance.
