# ARK Space & Defense Innovation ETF

**Website:** [ark-funds.com](https://ark-funds.com/funds/arkx/)
**Logo:** https://ark-funds.com/wp-content/themes/ark-funds/assets/images/logo.svg
**Type:** Aerospace & Defense ETF (Defense)
**Country:** USA

The ARK Space & Defense Innovation ETF (ARKX) is an actively managed exchange-traded fund that targets companies leading, enabling, or benefiting from technologically enabled products and services in space exploration and advanced defense. The fund's strategy focuses on disruptive innovation across orbital and sub-orbital aerospace, autonomous mobility, and "enabling technologies" such as artificial intelligence, robotics, and 3D printing, providing investors with exposure to the future of global security and the final frontier.
