# NuScale Power Corporation

**Website:** [nuscalepower.com](https://www.nuscalepower.com)
**Logo:** https://www.nuscalepower.com/wp-content/themes/nuscale/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Small Modular Reactor (SMR) Technology (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

NuScale Power is a pioneer in Small Modular Reactor (SMR) technology and was the first company to receive design approval from the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC). Its VOYGR SMR design is intended to provide a scalable, safe, and cost-effective alternative to traditional large-scale nuclear plants. NuScale is at the forefront of the SMR commercialization wave, targeting the growing global demand for flexible carbon-free baseload power.
