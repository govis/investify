# Saab AB

**Website:** [saab.com](https://www.saab.com)
**Logo:** https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/Saab_Logo.svg/1280px-Saab_Logo.svg.png
**Type:** Defense & Aerospace (Defense)
**Country:** Sweden

Saab AB is a leading Swedish aerospace and defense company that provides advanced technology solutions for military defense and civil security. Founded to secure Sweden's domestic aircraft production, it has evolved into a global prime contractor. Its operations are divided into several key business areas: Aeronautics (Gripen fighter jet), Dynamics (ground combat weapons like NLAW and Carl-Gustaf), Surveillance (radar and electronic warfare), and Kockums (submarines and naval systems). Saab is a primary beneficiary of the structural shift in European defense spending and Sweden's integration into the NATO defense framework.
