# Laramide Resources Ltd.

**Website:** [laramide.com](https://www.laramide.com)
**Logo:** https://laramide.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration & Development (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

Laramide Resources holds a portfolio of advanced uranium projects located in Tier-1 jurisdictions, primarily in Australia and the United States. Its key assets include the Westmoreland project in Queensland, Australia, and the Churchrock and Crownpoint projects in New Mexico, USA. Laramide is focused on advancing these projects toward production to meet the growing need for secure, Western-sourced uranium for the nuclear industry.
