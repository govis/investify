# Atha Energy Corp.

**Website:** [athaenergy.com](https://www.athaenergy.com)
**Logo:** https://www.athaenergy.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

Atha Energy is a Canadian mineral exploration company focused on the acquisition and development of uranium assets in world-class jurisdictions. The company holds the largest cumulative exploration land package in Canada's Athabasca and Thelon Basins, two of the most significant districts for high-grade uranium. Atha Energy is dedicated to systematic exploration and discovery to provide a secure and long-term supply of uranium for the global clean energy transition.
