# Skyharbour Resources Ltd.

**Website:** [skyharbourltd.com](https://www.skyharbourltd.com)
**Logo:** https://skyharbourltd.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

Skyharbour Resources is a Canadian uranium and thorium exploration company with an extensive portfolio of projects in the Athabasca Basin, Saskatchewan. The company employs a "prospect generator" model, advancing its secondary assets through partner-funded exploration while maintaining high-impact focus on its core projects, such as Moore Uranium and Russell Lake. Skyharbour provides investors with broad exposure to high-grade uranium targets in a premier mining jurisdiction.
