# Rolls-Royce Holdings plc

**Website:** [rolls-royce.com](https://www.rolls-royce.com)
**Logo:** https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/d/df/Rolls-Royce_Holdings_logo.svg/1280px-Rolls-Royce_Holdings_logo.svg.png
**Type:** Aerospace & Defense / Power Systems (Defense & Nuclear)
**Country:** UK

Rolls-Royce Holdings plc is a pre-eminent British multinational engineering company that designs, manufactures, and services power systems for air, land, and sea. It is a global leader in civil aerospace, defense, and power systems. Rolls-Royce is a critical partner for global defense, providing engines for military aircraft and naval vessels. Furthermore, it is the sole provider of nuclear propulsion systems for the UK’s submarine fleet and a frontrunner in developing Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) for clean, baseload power.
