# CGN Mining Company Limited

**Website:** [cgnmc.com](http://www.cgnmc.com)
**Logo:** https://www.logo.wine/a/logo/China_General_Nuclear_Power_Group/China_General_Nuclear_Power_Group-Logo.wine.svg
**Type:** Uranium Mining & Trading (Nuclear)
**Country:** China / Hong Kong

CGN Mining Company Limited is the primary investment and financing platform for overseas uranium resources under China General Nuclear Power Group (CGN), China's largest nuclear power operator. The company is an integrated uranium supplier focused on the development and trading of natural uranium, holding significant stakes in low-cost In-Situ Recovery (ISR) mines in Kazakhstan. As the only listed uranium arm of CGN, it serves as a strategic vehicle to secure long-term uranium supply for China's massive nuclear energy expansion and provides investors with pure-play exposure to the global uranium market.
