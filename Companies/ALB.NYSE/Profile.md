# Albemarle Corporation

**Website:** [albemarle.com](https://www.albemarle.com)
**Logo:** https://www.albemarle.com/themes/custom/albemarle/logo.svg
**Type:** Lithium & Specialty Chemicals (Electrification)
**Country:** USA

Albemarle is a global leader in the production of lithium and bromine, essential materials for various industrial and consumer applications. As the primary supplier of lithium for electric vehicle batteries, Albemarle is at the heart of the global electrification cycle. The company operates high-quality lithium assets in the USA, Chile, and Australia, and maintains long-term offtake agreements with major battery and EV manufacturers.
