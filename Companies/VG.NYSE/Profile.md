# Venture Global LNG

**Website:** [ventureglobal.com](https://ventureglobal.com)
**Logo:** https://ventureglobal.com/wp-content/themes/venture-global/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** LNG Production & Export (Reshoring)
**Country:** USA

Venture Global LNG is a long-term, low-cost provider of American liquefied natural gas. The company utilizes a highly efficient modular construction process to develop its export facilities, including Calcasieu Pass and Plaquemines LNG. Venture Global is a major disruptor in the LNG market, focusing on meeting the growing global demand for secure and affordable energy through massive capacity expansion and innovative financing.
