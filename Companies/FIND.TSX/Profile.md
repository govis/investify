# Baselode Energy Corp.

**Website:** [baselode.com](https://baselode.com)
**Logo:** https://baselode.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

Baselode Energy is a Canadian uranium exploration company focused on identifying high-grade, near-surface, basement-hosted uranium deposits in the Athabasca Basin, Saskatchewan. Utilizing their "Athabasca 2.0" exploration model, Baselode targets structurally controlled deposits outside of the traditional sandstone-hosted corridors. In late 2025, Baselode announced a strategic merger with Forum Energy Metals to form Geiger Energy, further consolidating its position as a leading explorer in the basin.
