# Northrop Grumman Corporation

**Website:** [northropgrumman.com](https://www.northropgrumman.com)
**Logo:** https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Northrop_Grumman_logo_blue-on-clear_2020.svg/1280px-Northrop_Grumman_logo_blue-on-clear_2020.svg.png
**Type:** Aerospace & Defense Prime (Defense)
**Country:** USA

Northrop Grumman is a leading global aerospace and defense technology company. It operates through four primary segments: Aeronautics Systems (aircraft and autonomous systems), Defense Systems (missiles and tactical weapons), Mission Systems (cyber, radar, and sensors), and Space Systems (satellites and launch vehicles). The company is a key contractor for the U.S. Department of Defense and international allies, known for high-profile programs like the B-21 Raider stealth bomber and the Sentinel (GBSD) ICBM modernization program. Northrop Grumman is positioned as a pure-play defense leader with significant exposure to high-priority, long-cycle national security programs and advanced space technologies.
