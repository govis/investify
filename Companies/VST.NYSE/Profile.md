# Vistra Corp.

**Website:** [vistracorp.com](https://www.vistracorp.com)
**Logo:** https://www.vistracorp.com/wp-content/themes/vistra/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Nuclear Utility & Integrated Power (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

Vistra Corp. is an integrated retail electricity and power generation company with a diverse portfolio that includes a significant nuclear fleet, most notably the Comanche Peak Nuclear Power Plant. Following its acquisition of Energy Harbor, Vistra has become one of the largest competitive nuclear operators in the U.S. The company is well-positioned to capture the growing demand for reliable "firm" power from data centers and industrial customers as part of the electrification and nuclear renaissance themes.
