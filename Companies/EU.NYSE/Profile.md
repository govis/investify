# enCore Energy Corp.

**Website:** [encoreuranium.com](https://www.encoreuranium.com)
**Logo:** https://www.encoreuranium.com/wp-content/themes/encore/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Mining (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

enCore Energy is the most diversified In-Situ Recovery (ISR) uranium development company in the United States. Focused on becoming the next primary U.S. uranium producer, the company is advancing a portfolio of production-ready assets in Texas, Wyoming, and South Dakota. enCore's "hub-and-spoke" production strategy and its focus on domestic supply make it a key beneficiary of the U.S. government's efforts to secure the nuclear fuel supply chain.
