# Azincourt Energy Corp.

**Website:** [azincourtenergy.com](https://azincourtenergy.com)
**Logo:** https://azincourtenergy.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration & Development (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

Azincourt Energy is a Canadian resource company specializing in the strategic acquisition, exploration, and development of alternative energy projects, with a primary focus on uranium. Its flagship asset is the East Preston project, located in the world-class Athabasca Basin, Saskatchewan. Azincourt is dedicated to discovering high-grade uranium deposits to support the global transition toward clean, carbon-free nuclear energy.
