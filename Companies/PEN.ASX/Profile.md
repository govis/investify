# Peninsula Energy Limited

**Website:** [pel.net.au](https://www.pel.net.au)
**Logo:** https://www.pel.net.au/wp-content/themes/peninsula/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Mining & Exploration (Nuclear)
**Country:** Australia

Peninsula Energy is an Australian-listed company focused on its 100%-owned Lance Uranium Project in Wyoming, USA. Lance is one of the largest uranium ISR (in-situ recovery) projects in the United States. Peninsula is transitioning to low-pH ISR technology to improve operational efficiency and lower production costs, positioning itself as a significant and reliable domestic supplier of uranium for the U.S. nuclear utility market.
