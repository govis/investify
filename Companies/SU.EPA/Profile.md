# Schneider Electric SE

**Website:** [se.com](https://www.se.com)
**Logo:** https://www.se.com/ww/en/assets/images/logo/schneider-electric-logo.svg
**Type:** Energy Management & Industrial Automation (Electrification)
**Country:** France

Schneider Electric is a global leader in energy management and industrial automation. The company provides integrated solutions for homes, buildings, data centers, and industries, combining energy technologies, real-time automation, software, and services. Schneider Electric is a key enabler of the electrification super-cycle, particularly through its "EcoStruxure" platform which helps organizations optimize energy use and integrate renewable energy sources.
