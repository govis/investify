# BHP Group Limited

**Website:** [bhp.com](https://www.bhp.com)
**Logo:** https://www.bhp.com/-/media/project/bhp1p/bhp-com-iron/images/logo/bhp-logo.png
**Type:** Diversified Mining / Copper & Potash (Electrification)
**Country:** Australia

BHP is one of the world's largest diversified mining companies, with a significant focus on copper, iron ore, and nickel. It operates major assets like the Escondida copper mine in Chile and the Olympic Dam mine in Australia. BHP is strategically pivoting toward "future-facing" commodities, particularly copper, which is essential for global electrification and the renewable energy transition.
