# Rio Tinto Group

**Website:** [riotinto.com](https://www.riotinto.com)
**Logo:** https://www.riotinto.com/static/images/logo.svg
**Type:** Diversified Mining / Copper & Lithium (Electrification)
**Country:** UK / Australia

Rio Tinto is a leading global mining group that produces iron ore, aluminium, copper, and diamonds. The company is aggressively expanding its footprint in critical minerals like lithium and copper to support the global energy transition. Rio Tinto’s focus on low-carbon aluminium and its significant copper growth pipeline make it a key player in the electrification investment theme.
