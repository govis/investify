# Lundin Mining Corporation

**Website:** [lundinmining.com](https://www.lundinmining.com)
**Logo:** https://www.lundinmining.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Base Metals Mining / Copper (Electrification)
**Country:** Canada

Lundin Mining is a diversified Canadian base metals mining company with operations in Chile, Brazil, Portugal, Sweden, and the USA. The company primarily produces copper, zinc, gold, and nickel from high-quality assets. Lundin Mining is focused on operating in "safe" jurisdictions and maintaining a strong balance sheet, providing institutional investors with reliable exposure to the copper-driven electrification super-cycle.
