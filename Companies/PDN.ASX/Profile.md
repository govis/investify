# Paladin Energy Ltd

**Website:** [paladinenergy.com.au](https://www.paladinenergy.com.au)
**Logo:** https://www.paladinenergy.com.au/wp-content/themes/paladin/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Mining (Nuclear)
**Country:** Australia

Paladin Energy is an independent uranium producer that owns the Langer Heinrich Mine in Namibia, one of the world's most significant uranium assets. The company recently restarted production at Langer Heinrich to capture value from the structural deficit in the uranium market. Paladin also holds a suite of high-quality exploration and development assets in Australia and Canada, providing diversified exposure to the global nuclear fuel supply chain.
