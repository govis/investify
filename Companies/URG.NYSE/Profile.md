# Ur-Energy Inc.

**Website:** [ur-energy.com](https://www.ur-energy.com)
**Logo:** https://www.ur-energy.com/wp-content/themes/urenergy/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Mining (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

Ur-Energy is a U.S. uranium mining company that operates the Lost Creek in-situ recovery (ISR) facility in Wyoming. Lost Creek is one of the lowest-cost and most efficient uranium mines in the United States. Ur-Energy is also advancing its Shirley Basin project, aiming to expand its domestic production capacity to meet the rising demand for secure, American-sourced uranium for the nuclear utility sector.
