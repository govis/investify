# BAE Systems plc

**Website:** [baesystems.com](https://www.baesystems.com)
**Logo:** https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/0/02/BAE_Systems_logo.svg/1200px-BAE_Systems_logo.svg.png
**Type:** Aerospace & Defense Prime (Defense)
**Country:** UK

BAE Systems is a British multinational aerospace, arms, and information security company, and the largest defense contractor in Europe. Its operations are divided into five primary segments: Air (Eurofighter Typhoon, F-35 components), Maritime (submarines and frigates), Electronic Systems, Platforms & Services (combat vehicles and munitions), and Cyber & Intelligence. As a critical Tier 1 partner to the UK Ministry of Defence and the U.S. Department of Defense, BAE Systems benefits from multi-decade government contracts and a massive order backlog, positioned at the forefront of global defense modernization.
