# Freeport-McMoRan Inc.

**Website:** [fcx.com](https://www.fcx.com)
**Logo:** https://www.fcx.com/themes/custom/fcx/logo.svg
**Type:** Copper & Molybdenum Mining (Electrification)
**Country:** USA

Freeport-McMoRan is a leading international mining company and the world's largest publicly traded copper producer. It operates significant assets including the Grasberg minerals district in Indonesia and several large-scale mines in North and South America. As a pure-play copper giant with a robust growth pipeline, Freeport-McMoRan is a central vehicle for investors seeking exposure to the primary metal required for global electrification and digital infrastructure.
