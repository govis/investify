# Glencore plc

**Website:** [glencore.com](https://www.glencore.com)
**Logo:** https://www.glencore.com/assets/images/logo.svg
**Type:** Diversified Mining, Recycling & Trading (Electrification)
**Country:** Switzerland

Glencore is one of the world's largest diversified natural resource companies, producing and marketing over 60 commodities. The company is a major producer of copper, cobalt, and nickel, which are critical for battery technology and electrification. Glencore also operates a massive battery metal recycling and trading arm, providing a unique "circular economy" play within the energy transition theme. Its global scale and integrated business model allow it to capture value across the entire mineral supply chain.
