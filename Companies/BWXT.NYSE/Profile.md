# BWX Technologies, Inc.

**Website:** [bwxt.com](https://www.bwxt.com)
**Logo:** https://www.bwxt.com/static/images/bwxt-logo.png
**Type:** Nuclear Components & Fuel Manufacturing (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

BWX Technologies (BWXT) is a specialty manufacturer of nuclear components and fuel, primarily serving the U.S. government and the commercial nuclear power industry. The company is a key supplier for the Naval Nuclear Propulsion Program and provides critical engineering and manufacturing services for Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) and advanced nuclear technologies. With a massive backlog and a dominant position in nuclear engineering, BWXT is a central proxy for the Western onshoring of critical nuclear infrastructure.
