# Syrah Resources Limited

**Website:** [syrahresources.com.au](https://www.syrahresources.com.au)
**Logo:** https://www.syrahresources.com.au/static/media/logo.8093254c.svg
**Type:** Graphite Mining & Battery Material Technology (Reshoring)
**Country:** Australia

Syrah Resources is an industrial minerals and technology company that operates the Balama Graphite Operation in Mozambique, one of the world's largest natural graphite mines. It is also developing the Vidalia Active Anode Material facility in the USA, aiming to become a vertically integrated supplier of natural graphite anode material to Western battery manufacturers, validating the "Western Anode" thesis.
