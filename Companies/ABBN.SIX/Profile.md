# ABB Ltd

**Website:** [global.abb](https://global.abb)
**Logo:** https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/00/ABB_logo.svg/1280px-ABB_logo.svg.png
**Type:** Electrification & Automation (Electrification & AI)
**Country:** Switzerland

ABB is a leading global technology company that specializes in electrification and automation. The company operates across four main business areas: Electrification, Motion, Process Automation, and Robotics & Discrete Automation. ABB is a primary beneficiary of the global shift toward electrical energy and industrial digitalization. Its portfolio covers the entire electrical value chain and integrates artificial intelligence through the ABB Ability™ platform to optimize industrial productivity, energy efficiency, and sustainability for customers in the utility, industry, and infrastructure sectors.
