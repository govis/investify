# Fission Uranium Corp.

**Website:** [fissionuranium.com](https://www.fissionuranium.com)
**Logo:** https://fissionuranium.com/wp-content/themes/fission/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration & Development (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

Fission Uranium is a resource company focused on the strategic development of the Triple R uranium deposit at its Patterson Lake South (PLS) project in Canada's Athabasca Basin. Triple R is one of the largest high-grade, near-surface uranium deposits in the world. Recently acquired by Paladin Energy, Fission’s assets are a key component of a new global uranium champion, providing significant scale and high-grade production potential for the next decade.
