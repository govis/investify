# Siemens Energy AG

**Website:** [siemens-energy.com](https://www.siemens-energy.com)
**Logo:** https://www.siemens-energy.com/etc.clientlibs/siemens-energy/clientlibs/clientlib-site/resources/images/logo-se.svg
**Type:** Energy Technology & Grid Infrastructure (Electrification)
**Country:** Germany

Siemens Energy is a global leader in energy technology, providing solutions across the entire value chain. The company’s portfolio includes gas services, grid technologies, and wind power through its Siemens Gamesa business. Siemens Energy is at the forefront of the electrification super-cycle, offering essential technologies for smart grid management, transformer production, and high-efficiency industrial power solutions.
