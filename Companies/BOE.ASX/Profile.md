# Boss Energy Limited

**Website:** [bossenergy.com](https://www.bossenergy.com)
**Logo:** https://www.bossenergy.com/wp-content/themes/boss/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Mining (Nuclear)
**Country:** Australia

Boss Energy is an Australian uranium company focused on the restart of its 100%-owned Honeymoon Uranium Project in South Australia. Honeymoon is a low-cost in-situ recovery (ISR) project with a fast-track to production. Boss Energy also holds a 30% interest in the Alta Mesa ISR project in Texas, USA, providing strategic geographic diversification and positioning the company as a key near-term producer in the global nuclear renaissance.
