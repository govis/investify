# NexGen Energy Ltd.

**Website:** [nexgenenergy.ca](https://www.nexgenenergy.ca)
**Logo:** https://www.nexgenenergy.ca/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration & Development (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

NexGen Energy is a leading Canadian company focused on the development of the Rook I Project in the Athabasca Basin, Saskatchewan. Rook I hosts the world-class Arrow Deposit, which is one of the largest and highest-grade undeveloped uranium resources globally. NexGen is committed to delivering a new standard for elite mining through high ESG performance and is positioned as a future cornerstone supplier for the global nuclear power industry.
