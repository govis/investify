# Toro Energy Limited

**Website:** [toroenergy.com.au](https://www.toroenergy.com.au)
**Logo:** https://www.toroenergy.com.au/wp-content/themes/toroenergy/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Development & Exploration (Nuclear)
**Country:** Australia

Toro Energy is an Australian uranium development and exploration company. Its flagship asset is the Wiluna Uranium Project in Western Australia, which consists of several high-grade deposits including Centipede-Millipede, Lake Maitland, and Lake Way. Toro Energy is one of the few Australian uranium projects with both Federal and State environmental approvals in place. In early 2026, the company entered into a merger agreement with IsoEnergy Ltd, positioned to create a top-tier global uranium developer to capitalize on the rising global demand for low-carbon baseload power.
