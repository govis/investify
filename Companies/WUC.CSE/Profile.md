# Western Uranium & Vanadium Corp.

**Website:** [western-uranium.com](https://www.western-uranium.com)
**Logo:** https://www.western-uranium.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium & Vanadium Mining (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA / Canada

Western Uranium & Vanadium Corp. is a near-term producer focused on uranium and vanadium assets in the United States. The company operates several mines in the Uravan Mineral Belt of Colorado and Utah, including the flagship Sunday Mine Complex. With a focus on the domestic supply of critical energy minerals, Western Uranium is well-positioned to benefit from the growing demand for nuclear fuel and the expansion of the vanadium battery market for long-duration energy storage.
