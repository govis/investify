# Denison Mines Corp.

**Website:** [denisonmines.com](https://www.denisonmines.com)
**Logo:** https://denisonmines.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Mining & Development (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

Denison Mines is a uranium exploration and development company with a primary focus on the Athabasca Basin region of northern Saskatchewan. Its flagship asset is a 95% interest in the Wheeler River Project, which is the largest undeveloped high-grade uranium project in the infrastructure-rich eastern portion of the basin. Denison is a pioneer in applying in-situ recovery (ISR) mining technology to high-grade uranium deposits, aiming for industry-leading low costs and minimal environmental impact.
