# IsoEnergy Ltd.

**Website:** [isoenergy.ca](https://www.isoenergy.ca)
**Logo:** https://www.isoenergy.ca/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration & Development (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

IsoEnergy is a Canadian company focused on the discovery and development of high-grade uranium projects in the Athabasca Basin, Saskatchewan. Its cornerstone asset is the Hurricane deposit at the Larocque East project, which hosts some of the highest uranium grades ever discovered globally. IsoEnergy is backed by a strong technical team and strategic shareholders, positioning it as a high-beta vehicle for exploration success and resource growth in the world's premier uranium district.
