# Stallion Uranium Corp.

**Website:** [stallionuranium.com](https://www.stallionuranium.com)
**Logo:** https://www.stallionuranium.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

Stallion Uranium is a Canadian exploration company targeting high-grade uranium discoveries in the world-renowned Athabasca Basin. The company holds a significant land position in the southwestern portion of the basin, adjacent to major discoveries. Stallion utilizes advanced geophysical and geological techniques to identify Tier-1 uranium targets, aiming to contribute to the global supply of carbon-free energy through domestic exploration success.
