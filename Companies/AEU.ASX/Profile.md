# Atomic Eagle Limited

**Website:** [atomiceagle.com.au](https://www.atomiceagle.com.au)
**Logo:** https://www.atomiceagle.com.au/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration & Development (Nuclear)
**Country:** Australia

Atomic Eagle Limited is an Australian-listed uranium developer focused on its 100%-owned Muntanga Uranium Project in southern Zambia. The project hosts significant JORC-compliant uranium resources across several deposits. Formed through a strategic transition from its iron ore roots, Atomic Eagle is now dedicated to expanding its resource base and becoming a key supplier for the global nuclear fuel market in response to the rising demand for carbon-free baseload power.
