# Cameco Corporation

**Website:** [cameco.com](https://www.cameco.com)
**Logo:** https://www.cameco.com/img/cameco-logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Mining & Nuclear Fuel Services (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

Cameco is one of the world's largest global providers of uranium fuel and nuclear fuel services. The company operates high-grade uranium mines in Canada (McArthur River and Cigar Lake) and has a significant stake in the Westinghouse Electric Company, a leading nuclear services provider. Cameco is a foundational anchor for the nuclear renaissance, offering integrated solutions across the entire fuel cycle to meet the growing global demand for carbon-free baseload power.
