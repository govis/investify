# Centrus Energy Corp.

**Website:** [centrusenergy.com](https://www.centrusenergy.com)
**Logo:** https://www.centrusenergy.com/wp-content/themes/centrus/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Nuclear Fuel Cycle & Enrichment (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

Centrus Energy is a leading supplier of nuclear fuel and services for the nuclear power industry. The company is the sole U.S. licensee for the production of High-Assay Low-Enriched Uranium (HALEU), which is essential for the next generation of advanced nuclear reactors and Small Modular Reactors (SMRs). Centrus is a direct beneficiary of the global shift toward secure, Western-allied enrichment capacity and the ongoing decoupling from Russian nuclear fuel supplies.
