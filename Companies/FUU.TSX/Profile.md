# F3 Uranium Corp.

**Website:** [f3uranium.com](https://f3uranium.com)
**Logo:** https://f3uranium.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

F3 Uranium is a Canadian uranium exploration company focused on the discovery and development of high-grade uranium projects in the Athabasca Basin, Saskatchewan. The company is best known for the high-grade JR Zone discovery at its Patterson Lake North (PLN) project. F3 is led by an experienced geological team with a proven track record of finding major deposits, positioning the company as a high-impact explorer in the global nuclear renaissance.
