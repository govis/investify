# Pilbara Minerals Limited

**Website:** [pilbaraminerals.com.au](https://www.pilbaraminerals.com.au)
**Logo:** https://www.pilbaraminerals.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/Pilbara-Minerals-Logo.png
**Type:** Lithium Mining & Processing (Electrification)
**Country:** Australia

Pilbara Minerals is the leading ASX-listed pure-play lithium company. It owns and operates the world-class Pilgangoora Operation in Western Australia, one of the largest independent hard-rock lithium deposits globally. Pilbara Minerals is strategically expanding its production capacity and moving downstream through joint ventures for lithium chemical conversion, positioning itself as a major long-term partner for the global battery supply chain.
