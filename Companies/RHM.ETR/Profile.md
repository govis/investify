# Rheinmetall AG

**Website:** [rheinmetall.com](https://www.rheinmetall.com)
**Logo:** https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/ca/Rheinmetall_Logo.svg/1280px-Rheinmetall_Logo.svg.png
**Type:** Defense & Automotive Systems (Defense)
**Country:** Germany

Rheinmetall AG is a leading international systems supplier in the defense industry and a major player in the automotive sector. As Germany's largest arms manufacturer, it specializes in military vehicle systems, weapons and ammunition, electronic solutions, and power systems. The company is a primary beneficiary of the European rearmament super-cycle, providing critical equipment such as Leopard 2 tank components, Lynx infantry fighting vehicles, and advanced air defense systems to NATO and EU member states.
