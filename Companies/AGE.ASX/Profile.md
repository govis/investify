# Alligator Energy Limited

**Website:** [alligatorenergy.com.au](https://www.alligatorenergy.com.au)
**Logo:** https://www.alligatorenergy.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Alligator-Energy-Logo-Final-Full-Colour.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration & Development (Nuclear)
**Country:** Australia

Alligator Energy is an Australian exploration and development company focused on uranium and energy-related metals essential for the transition to carbon-free energy. The company's flagship asset is the Samphire Uranium Project in South Australia, which is being developed as a low-cost In-Situ Recovery (ISR) operation. Alligator Energy also holds significant land positions in the world-class Alligator Rivers Uranium Province in the Northern Territory and the Big Lake Uranium project in the Cooper Basin, providing investors with a balanced portfolio of advanced development and high-impact exploration assets in tier-one jurisdictions.
