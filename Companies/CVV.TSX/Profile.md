# CanAlaska Uranium Ltd.

**Website:** [canalaska.com](https://www.canalaska.com)
**Logo:** https://canalaska.com/site/assets/files/1/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium & Nickel Exploration (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

CanAlaska Uranium is a Canadian "project generator" and active explorer holding one of the largest land positions in the Athabasca Basin, Saskatchewan—the world's premier district for high-grade uranium. The company manages a diverse portfolio of uranium, nickel, and diamond projects. By partnering with major mining companies to fund exploration, CanAlaska reduces financial risk while maintaining significant upside in multiple Tier-1 targets, including the high-grade Pike Zone discovery at its West McArthur project.
