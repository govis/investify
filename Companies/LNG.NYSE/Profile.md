# Cheniere Energy, Inc.

**Website:** [cheniere.com](https://www.cheniere.com)
**Logo:** https://www.cheniere.com/themes/custom/cheniere/logo.svg
**Type:** LNG Infrastructure & Export (Reshoring)
**Country:** USA

Cheniere Energy is the leading producer and exporter of liquefied natural gas (LNG) in the United States. It operates two of the largest LNG export facilities in the world—Sabine Pass and Corpus Christi—providing a reliable energy lifeline to global markets. As a major player in the "Reshoring Basket," Cheniere benefits from the global shift toward secure, Western-allied energy supply chains.
