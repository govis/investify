# Global Atomic Corporation

**Website:** [globalatomiccorp.com](https://www.globalatomiccorp.com)
**Logo:** https://globalatomiccorp.com/wp-content/themes/globalatomic/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Development & Zinc Production (Nuclear)
**Country:** Canada

Global Atomic is a unique energy and minerals company developing the high-grade Dasa uranium deposit in Niger. Dasa is the largest high-grade uranium discovery in Africa in decades and is being fast-tracked toward production. Additionally, Global Atomic generates steady cash flow from its 49% interest in a modern zinc recycling joint venture in Turkey. This dual-asset model provides a unique combination of growth and stability within the nuclear and industrial mineral sectors.
