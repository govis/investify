# Constellation Energy Corporation

**Website:** [constellationenergy.com](https://www.constellationenergy.com)
**Logo:** https://www.constellationenergy.com/content/dam/constellation/logos/constellation-logo.svg
**Type:** Nuclear Utility & Clean Energy Production (Nuclear)
**Country:** USA

Constellation Energy is the largest producer of carbon-free energy in the United States and the leading operator of commercial nuclear power plants. Providing approximately 10% of the nation's clean energy, Constellation is a foundational anchor for the nuclear renaissance. The company maintains a massive fleet of high-uptime nuclear assets and is a preferred partner for technology hyperscalers (such as Microsoft and Meta) seeking reliable, 24/7 carbon-free power through long-term purchase agreements.
