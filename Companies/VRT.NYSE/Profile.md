# Vertiv Holdings Co.

**Website:** [vertiv.com](https://www.vertiv.com)
**Logo:** https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cd/Vertiv_logo.svg/1280px-Vertiv_logo.svg.png
**Type:** AI Infrastructure / Data Center Hardware (AI)
**Country:** USA

Vertiv is a global leader in providing mission-critical digital infrastructure technologies and lifecycle services. The company designs, builds, and services the hardware, software, and analytics that ensure continuous operation for data centers, communication networks, and commercial/industrial facilities. Vertiv is a primary beneficiary of the AI super-cycle, providing essential power management and liquid cooling solutions that enable high-density AI computing. With a record order backlog and strategic partnerships with major chipmakers and hyperscalers, Vertiv is at the forefront of the "grid-to-chip" infrastructure transition.
