# Norsk Hydro ASA

**Website:** [hydro.com](https://www.hydro.com)
**Logo:** https://www.hydro.com/Static/img/hydro-logo.svg
**Type:** Low-Carbon Aluminium & Renewable Energy (Electrification)
**Country:** Norway

Norsk Hydro is one of the world's largest aluminium companies, uniquely integrated across the entire value chain from bauxite mining to primary metal production and recycling. Powered by its own hydroelectric energy, Norsk Hydro is a critical supplier of low-carbon aluminium, which is essential for the lightweighting of electric vehicles and the expansion of global electricity grids. The company’s focus on sustainability makes it a preferred provider for the "green" infrastructure required by the electrification thesis.
