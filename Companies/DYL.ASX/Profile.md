# Deep Yellow Limited

**Website:** [deepyellow.com.au](https://www.deepyellow.com.au)
**Logo:** https://deepyellow.com.au/wp-content/themes/deepyellow/assets/images/logo.png
**Type:** Uranium Exploration & Development (Nuclear)
**Country:** Australia

Deep Yellow is an Australian uranium company with a dual-pillar growth strategy focused on Namibia and Western Australia. The company is advancing the Tumas Project in Namibia toward production and holds the Mulga Rock Project in Western Australia. Led by an experienced team with a track record of success in the uranium sector, Deep Yellow aims to become a significant, multi-mine uranium producer to meet the growing global demand for carbon-free energy.
